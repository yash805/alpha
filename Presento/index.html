Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the process for creating a website with your agency?

    Our website creation process typically involves several stages, including initial consultation, design mockups, development, testing, and deployment. We work closely with clients to ensure their vision is realized at every step.

  • How long does it typically take to complete a website project?

    The timeline for website projects can vary depending on factors such as complexity, features, and client feedback. On average, a basic website can take around 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects may take several months.

  • What platform or technologies do you use to build websites?

    We are proficient in a variety of platforms and technologies, including WordPress, Drupal, Joomla, and custom-built solutions using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other programming languages.

  • Do you offer custom design services, or do you use templates?

    We offer both custom design services and the option to use templates, depending on the client's preferences and budget. Our team can create unique designs tailored to your brand identity or customize existing templates to meet your needs.

  • Can you provide examples of websites you've created in the past?

    Yes, we have a portfolio of past projects that showcase our work. You can view examples of websites we've created on our website or request specific case studies relevant to your industry.

  • Do you offer website maintenance and support services after the site is launched?

    Yes, we provide website maintenance and support services to ensure your site remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally after launch. Our maintenance plans include regular backups, security updates, and technical support.
